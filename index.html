The Benefits of Playing a Musical Instrument

It is the brains equivalent to a
full body workout

It engages almost every part of the brain, especially the visual, auditory, and
motor cortex

It strengthens brain functions that can be applied elsewhere, such as solving puzzles and problems effectively and creatively

It enhances memory functions

It has been shown to help children with learning disabilities

Not only does playing an instrument have all of these benefits, but it is fun and a great creative outlet!

Now that you know how beneficial it is, go pick up that instrument you have always wanted to learn and lets make sure we keep music education in our schools!
